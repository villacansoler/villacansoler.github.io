Welcome home

Simple pleasures,
beautifully placed.

Can Soler is our family house in the north of Mallorca — a warm, relaxed place where days unfold naturally between the pool, the terrace and the nearby Mediterranean.

Set close to Alcúdia's beaches and historic old town, it is a home for gathering, resting and discovering the island at your own pace.

8 guests 4 bedrooms 2 bathrooms 1 private pool
