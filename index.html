Where to eat

Bistro Mar

If you are feeling like eating a good paella, that's a great place. You can either eat there or pick it up to eat at home, which is what our family sometimes do on Sundays. There are also a good variety of dishes including great fresh fish.

Local 3, Passeig Marítim, Port d'Alcúdia Visit their website

Maca de Castro

Michelin starred restaurant where you can taste local Mallorcan cuisine at its best.

Carrer Tritons, 4, Alcudia Visit their website

La Terraza

Near the house, La Terraza offers a terrace next to the sea where you can eat meat, fish or rice or, simply take some cocktails while enjoying the views

Carrer Tritons, 4, Alcudia Visit their website

La Casa Gallega

A good place to eat quality tapas as well as closed menus during weekdays or weekends.

Carrer de l’Hostaleria, 11, Alcúdia Visit their website

Carbón Moreno

Burger joint in the city of Alcúdia where you can enjoy gourmet burgers in a relaxed atmosphere.

Passeig de la Mare de Déu de la Victòria, 4, Alcúdia Visit their website

Es Racó de Ronda

Right next to the old Alcudia walls, Es Racó de Ronda offers a picturesque space to take a bear, a wine or to have some local cold meats or cheese for dinner.

Camí de Ronda, 15, Alcúdia Visit their website

Can Punyetes

In the heart of Puerto de Alcúdia, Can Pucyetes offers a wide variety of traditional tapas in an informal atmosphere

Carrer de les Barques, 1, Port d’Alcúdia Visit their website

The Wine Side

A good place to have an international meal in the middle of Puerto de Alcúdia. You can also enjoy good wines there.

Carrer Mariners, 3, Port d’Alcúdia Visit their website

Forn Can Joan Serra

Local oven where you can buy local pastries or bread. There are also some typical cold meals like panades or espinagades.

Carrer dels Pins, 2, Port d'Alcudia Visit their website
